A little about Stoneyford

I live in the countryside outside Stoneyford Co. Kilkenny. Stoneyford is a nice little village located 20 minutes from Kilkenny City that has a small tight knit community that are very welcoming. I personally live outside Stoneyford in the contryside which I enjoy as the freedom of being allowed to explore nature and enjoy my hobbies without being hindered by the restricting nature of the city. I also enjoy how free of of noise and air polution that the countryside gives me which really adds to the relaxing experience.
